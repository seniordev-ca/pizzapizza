Membership Benefits

Club 11-11 is Pizza Pizza’s exclusive loyalty program!

Register your Pizza Pizza Club 11-11 Loyalty Card or any Pizza Pizza Gift Card at www.pizzapizza.ca and start earning!

Earn 5% in Loyalty ‘Dollars’ on every order.

Order only 5 times and redeem your Loyalty ‘Dollars’ after every 5th visit*

Order another 5 times in order to unlock your next 5% collected, and so on and so forth.

You must add your registered card to your online ordering account in order to earn Loyalty ‘Dollars’ on digital purchases.

*Loyalty ‘Dollars’ must be redeemed within 90 days after your 5th visit.

Protect your card if lost or stolen

Transfer your balance from your card to another

Access to manage your account online or on the Pizza Pizza app
